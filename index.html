01. About Me

Hello! I'm Hana, a passionate UI/UX Designer and web developer specializing in crafting modern, performant digital experiences that users love. With a keen eye for detail and a problem-solving mindset, I bridge the gap between design and functionality to build websites and applications that are not just visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible.

My journey in Tech began when I discovered the magic of turning lines of code into interactive realities. Since then, I'm constantly staying updated with industry trends to deliver cutting-edge solutions. Whether it's a sleek portfolio site, a robust e-commerce platform, or a dynamic web app, I approach every project with the same level of dedication and creativity.

Beyond Designing the products, writing clean, efficient code, I'm deeply committed to user-centered design. I believe technology should empower people, which is why I prioritize accessibility, responsiveness, and seamless user experiences in all my work.
